About Us

LightConSys® company is founded with a mission to provide an innovative control system for LED lighting, especially utilizing the latest IoT platform for better management and control of LED infrastructure installed. We believe the future of LED lighting control is based on prolonging its product life cycle, smarter environmental sensor integration, and widen the application use with customized software interfaces.

Light Management Software

LCS series devices can be managed and controlled by three types of light management software (or LMS software), the LMS EZ, Lite, and Pro. The LMS EZ is a simple to use web-based software for LED lighting control for scheduled ON/OFF and dimming. The LMS Lite is a client-based software with dashboard style graphic for quick viewing and controlling of LED lighting for scheduled ON/OFF, dimming, motion detection, and digital ON/OFF light switch function. The third and the most extensive control software for LED lighting is the LMS Pro. It is designed for controlling of larger scale of LED lighting installment. The LMS Pro allows complex scheduled ON/OFF, dimmer settings, and supports various sensor peripherals, making it an ultimate smart LED lighting control software for robust application requirement.
